The Tactical Pen

Self Defense Pens (also called "Tactical Pens") are practical defense tools. What appears at first to be a simple pen is revealed to be a well thought out defensive weapon capable of stopping attackers and, in the hands of the trained, an offensive tool capable of restraining and incapacitating. This is an excellent example of the pen being mightier than the sword in a literal sense.

×

The Blunt Edge

Something amazing about the tactical pen is that it has a blunt edge on one end of the pen.

This blunt edge can be used to help arm yourself in times of need, when either someone or something is trying to attack you.

×

The Glass Breaking Tip

The tip of the pen is also a very important and useful part of the tactical pen.
